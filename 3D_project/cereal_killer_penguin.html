Cereal Killer Penguins IN SPACE

Artist Statement

This project required us to use a 3D modeling platform known as TinkerCAD to make our own minature models. We were given the freedom to make whatever we wanted, and I knew I wanted a penguin. A classmate and I were talking and she was making cereal. Somehow we ended up with a cereal killer penguin, not sure when he went to space. His name is Private, however, he is not private about his insane bloodlust-like hunger for Honey Nut Cheerios.
